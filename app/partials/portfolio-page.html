Let There Be Light
Event Flyers. Photography by David Anthony.
January 09, 2011
I worked closely with Lumen and Effen Vodka to develop promotional graphics for a fashion event entitled Let There Be Light. Playing off of the elemental themes of the promo copy, we developed a series of four graphics for the event, each element represented by a different photograph. We were fortunate enough to work with David Anthony, one of Chicago's finest art and fashion photographers, to design graphics that were simple, eye-catching and clever. For the print promotions, we coupled the "element" on the front with a detail shot of the venue on the back that reflected that particular element. Can you tell?
